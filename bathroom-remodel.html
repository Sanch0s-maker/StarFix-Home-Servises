Scope of Work

What’s Included

A full bathroom remodel with StarFix covers every stage. One licensed contractor is responsible for all of it.

💥

Demolition

Old tile, tub, vanity and fixtures removed and hauled away. Floors and hallways protected throughout.

💧

Plumbing

Supply and drain lines, valves, and fixture rough-in. Relocation handled when the layout changes.

🛡

Waterproofing

Proper membrane and pan under every shower. This is the step that decides whether the work lasts.

🪟

Tile Work

Floors, walls, niches and curbs. Marble, subway, hexagon, pebble — set level with clean grout lines.

🛍

Vanity & Fixtures

Vanity, countertop, sink, faucet, toilet, mirror and hardware installed and sealed.

🚪

Glass Enclosure

Frameless and sliding glass shower doors measured, ordered and fitted after tile is complete.

🌪

Ventilation

Exhaust fan sized to the room and vented outside — the difference between a dry bathroom and mold.

🎨

Paint & Trim

Moisture-rated paint, baseboards, door casing and caulk lines finished to a clean edge.

Common Questions

Bathroom Remodeling FAQ

Straight answers about timelines, permits, materials and pricing.

Most full bathroom remodels run two to four weeks on site. Guest baths on the simpler end finish faster; primary baths with moved plumbing, custom tile or a glass enclosure take longer. Material lead times are usually the biggest variable, which is why we order everything before demolition starts.

If the layout stays the same and we are replacing fixtures in place, usually not. Once plumbing or electrical is moved, or a wall changes, a permit is required. As a licensed general contractor we pull the permit and handle the inspections — you do not deal with the city.

Either way works. Many clients pick tile, vanity and fixtures themselves and we install them. Others prefer we source everything. We will tell you honestly where spending more is worth it and where a cheaper option performs just as well.

Yes, in almost every case. We seal off the work area, protect floors and hallways, and clean up at the end of each day. If it is your only bathroom, we plan the schedule so the stretch without a working shower is as short as possible.

We price after seeing the space, never over the phone. Cost depends on the size of the room, whether plumbing moves, how complex the tile work is, and which fixtures you choose. You get a written itemized estimate before any work begins, and we do not add surprise change orders.
